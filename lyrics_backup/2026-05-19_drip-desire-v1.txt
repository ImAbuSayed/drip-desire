[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Kalli raat de hanere vich rooh chamke, pure magic
Naina cho pilave meetha zeher saanu, sweet poison
Mera dil tere piche gya rull kudiye, quite tragic
Teri adaa kardi deewana mainu sachi, lost emotion

[Hook 2]
Tere roop di chamak saanu maare seene, burning bright
Saari duniya bhulayi baithe yaar tere layi, through the night
Main udeek karda haan teri es raat vich, standing tall
Tere husn da jaadu pawe asar sadde te, catching the fall

[Verse 1]
Kach de glass vich kachian gallan mukk gaiyan, breaking ice
Main taan lutt gaya tere piche ajj sohniye, rolling dice
Teri mehak hawa vich ghuumdi ae thehar ke, midnight hour
Tere nakhre de agge haar gaya saara jagg, taking power
Thandi raat vich chalde ne saah mere kaahle, moving slow
Main taan mud mud vekha tera mukhra naseeb, watch you glow
Sade hosh udaye tere nakhre ne kudiye, true desire
Meri jaan kadd liti sohniye tu haske, catching fire

[Pre-Chorus]
Parinde wangu main ud javan asmaan vich, touching space
Saah rukde jado dekhan tera roop sohna, beautiful face
Kujh vi na disda mainu as-paas tere bin, losing control
Sohniye tu lutt layi sachi saddi duniya, my entire soul

[Chorus]
Ni tu agg wangu machdi phire hanere ch, burning flame
Mainu pagal kardi jaave saari raat nakhre, playing game
Meri jaan nikaldi jandi chup chap seene cho, wild romance
Tainu vekhi jaavan main khada reh ke kalla, in a trance
Hathan vich hath pa ke sohniye tu chal naal, step aside
Saari duniya bhulawan kalli tainu takk ke, for the ride
Main karda haan pyar tainu sachi dilon ajj, shining star
Tere bin reh nahi sakda main taan zindagi ch, near or far

[Verse 2]
Garam silencer cheekan marde ne sadkan te, engine roar
Piche udd da dhuwan vekhde ne vairi saare, wanting more
Pakki yaari saadi pathar wargi na tutdi kadi, moving fast
Main jittda haan sab apni himmat de naal, built to last
Tutya dil mera seene vich chubhda ae sachi, hiding pain
Ishq ch tere main rul gaya ajj kalla reh ke, in the rain
Koudi teri aadat da nasha ae mere jism te, toxic vibe
Tu hi ae meri saari kaynaat sohniye, only tribe
